There Will Come Soft Rains.

By: Ray Bradbury

In the living room the voice-clock sang, Tick-tock, seven o'clock, time to get up, time to get up, seven o'clock!

as if it were afraid that nobody would.

  • The morning house lay empty.
  • The clock ticked on,
  • repeating and repeating its sounds
  • into the emptiness.
